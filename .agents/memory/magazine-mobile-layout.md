---
name: Magazine mobile layout
description: Responsive design decision for the UJI magazine page and supplied editorial imagery.
---

The UJI magazine keeps its desktop editorial grid, while mobile intentionally switches to stacked content blocks with shorter image crops, readable Arabic copy, single-column article cards, and a vertical newsletter form. Supplied product-tin and tea-field imagery belong to the magazine editorial flow.

**Why:** The original magazine was composed for wide screens and became visually dense and inconsistent on phones.

**How to apply:** Preserve the desktop composition when extending the magazine, and add mobile-specific rules to the named magazine sections rather than shrinking the desktop grids proportionally.
